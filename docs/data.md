# Data Resources

This is a list of publicly available datasets from MS-based metabolomics studies conducted 
by our laboratory and contributed by users. All datasets are available under the 
CC-BY-NC v4.0 License. Each item includes sample information, experimental protocol, 
raw data, and saved analysis objects.

Contact jihongchao@caas.cn for dataset contribution.

### In-House Natural Product High-Resolution MS Database

Our lab has independently constructed a high-resolution mass spectrometry database covering 
**1,600 natural products**, with an additional ~4,000 metabolite standard MS spectra contributed 
by collaborating research groups. These resources support the validation of our computational 
methods for cross-source generalization.

*Access to this database is available upon request for collaborative research.*

## Public Data Resources Curated by Our Lab

For method development and benchmarking, we have systematically curated:

- **GNPS**: 26,000 compounds with 496,000 MS/MS spectra
- **NIST20**: 28,000 compounds with 932,000 EI-MS spectra

These datasets have been uniformly formatted, peak-filtered, and quality-controlled for 
training and testing our machine learning models.
