Contact jihongchao@caas.cn for dataset contribution.

In-House Natural Product High-Resolution MS Database

Our lab has independently constructed a high-resolution mass spectrometry database covering 1,600 natural products, with an additional ~4,000 metabolite standard MS spectra contributed by collaborating research groups. These resources support the validation of our computational methods for cross-source generalization.

Access to this database is available upon request for collaborative research.

Public Data Resources Curated by Our Lab

For method development and benchmarking, we have systematically curated:

  • GNPS: 26,000 compounds with 496,000 MS/MS spectra
  • NIST20: 28,000 compounds with 932,000 EI-MS spectra

These datasets have been uniformly formatted, peak-filtered, and quality-controlled for training and testing our machine learning models.
